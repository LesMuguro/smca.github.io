SMCA

SMCA, established in 2010, has been supplying non-ferrous metals to manufacturers within East Africa. Our products are sourced from Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

Products

We supply Lead, Copper, Zinc, and Aluzinc in jumbos and slabs.

Additionally, we offer specialty items like Fluospar, Bauxite (raw and calcined), Calcium Aluminate, Silicone Carbide, Graphite, Magnesia, and Tabular Alumina.

Plans to supply Aluminium jumbos, slabs, and AlSi are underway.
